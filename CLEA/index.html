Christ Love Evanelical Assembly [C.L.E.A]

Christ Love Evanelical Assembly [C.L.E.A] is a bible believing and word based community. A community of faith, hope, and love, where we come together to worship, pray, study the word, grow, and serve.

We're dedicated to spreading the good news of Jesus Christ and fostering a welcoming environment for all who seek to explore and deepen their relationship with God.

Our Vision

To become an equipping and mobilizing church that transforms, sustains and maintain people to be Christ centered and culturally relevant as the light and the salt that adds meaning and value to the kingdom of God and are a blessing to the local community.

People lost and broken by sin are being renewed by the covenant of the sacrifice of Jesus Christ on the cross of Calvary thereby making all things new and all things great for our community by Christ Jesus (Rev 21:5)

Our Beliefs

The Bible as God's Word : We hold the Bible as the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God. It serves as our guide for faith and practice in our daily walk with Go. Faith in the God-Head : We believe in Jesus Christ as the Son of God, our Lord, and Savior. His life, death, and resurrection provide the means for our salvation.

Prayer : Our services are dedicated to gear us to pray in the knowledge of Christ and by the Spirit. We understand that Prayer is an essential tool through which we edify the body of christ as well as communicate with God.

Our Pastorate

MISSION STATEMENT

Christ Love Evangelical Assembly is called to proclaim the Gospel of Christ and believes of the Evangelicals; to inspire all to live for Christ, passion for righteousness as well as consciousness of their duties to God and mankind.

ABOUT US

Christ Love Evangelical Assembly is a mission focused church based in Europe and Nigeria.
The registered trustees of the church in Nigeria include Pastor Mololouwa Patience Ojo, Mr. Ibukun Olaniyi Ojo, Mr Michael Kayode Alabi, Pastor Bolanle Oluwakemi Anyanwu.

The Senior Pastor of the Plearophoria Assembly which has transformed to the headquarter church at Adedeji Street is Apostle Babatope Olusegun Ojo. The Associate Pastor is Assistant Pastor Ekundayo Olusanjo Oginni. The church secretary is Brother Emmanuel Akinwunmi.
